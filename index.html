About The Author

Jackson Louthain was born in Michigan, but has lived in the Indianapolis area since his first birthday and believes this qualifies him as a Hoosier.

He is currently attending Avon Highschool as a Senior, and is a member of the boys tennis team. He has enjoyed his past five years on the team and is looking forward to his last season with Avon.

He is a bibliophile who's favorite genre is Fantasy. Over the past year he has been trying to get involved with both traditional writing and comics. Only to realize that his art is not yet up to par, which has slowed him down on the comic front.
